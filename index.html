Help & Support

How to Use

  1. On the 'Home' tab, select your country code.
  2. Enter your client's phone number (without the country code).
  3. Choose one of the three client categories. This choice is saved for future use.
  4. Tap the 'Go to WhatsApp' button.
  5. WhatsApp will open with the number and message pre-filled.
  6. IMPORTANT: You must manually attach your business card image before tapping 'Send'.
